WHO WE ARE

Pioneering the Future of Agri-Tech

TECHFARM Hub is a Pan-African social innovation and ecosystem-enabling hub dedicated to advancing entrepreneurship, innovation, and business development across Africa. We empower startups, entrepreneurs, and MSMEs by providing access to disruptive knowledge, modern tools, and practical skills that transform ideas into sustainable ventures. Our work places a strong emphasis on supporting youth, women, and Persons with Disabilities (PWDs), ensuring inclusivity and equal opportunity within the digital and agritech landscape.

At TECHFARM Hub, we serve as a catalyst for growth and collaboration—bridging the gap between technology, agriculture, and entrepreneurship. Through our training programs, innovation challenges, mentorship initiatives, and startup acceleration support, we nurture talent and cultivate a culture of creativity and problem-solving.

We are driven by a vision to unlock Africa’s potential through green, sustainable, and technology-driven agribusiness solutions. By stimulating enthusiasm for productive and profitable agritech and agribusiness ventures in Ghana and the diaspora, TECHFARM Hub continues to inspire a new generation of innovators ready to shape the future of agriculture and digital transformation across the continent.

Mission & Values That Drive Us

Our Mission

To provide a safe, resourceful and empowering space for young agripreneurs, innovators and startups and MSMEs to flourish disruptive ideas, upscale various innovative solutions, access funding and markets accelerate growth into productive and profitable ventures.

Our Vision

To become an industry leader building the next generation of business magnates to boost growth in emerging economies in Africa. Introduce tailored programs, projects trainings and support services in businessdevelopment, formalization, incubation, acceleration, investment readiness,match -making and mentorships.

Our Promise

To deliver innovative, practical, and sustainable agri-tech solutions that transform farming practices and create lasting impact for communities and the environment.

OUR HISTORY

Milestones in Our Journey

From humble beginnings to a global agri-tech innovation hub

2018

Foundation

TECHFARM Hub was founded by a group of agricultural experts and technology enthusiasts who saw the potential for innovation in the farming sector.

2019

First Accelerator Program

Launched our first accelerator program, supporting 12 promising agri-tech startups with funding, mentorship, and resources.

2020

Global Expansion

Expanded our operations to three new countries and established partnerships with leading agricultural universities and research institutions.

2021

$10M Funding Milestone

Reached a significant milestone by deploying over $10M in funding to agri-tech startups and innovation projects.

2022

Digital Platform Launch

Launched our digital learning platform, making our training programs accessible to aspiring agri-tech innovators worldwide.

2023

150+ Startups Supported

Celebrated supporting over 150 startups and training 2,000+ professionals in agri-tech innovation and sustainable practices.
