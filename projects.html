AfCFTA Campus Connect: Empowering the Next Generation

Launched: March 2022 8 min read

Project Overview

The AfCFTA Campus Connect initiative represents a strategic approach to preparing Ghanaian youth for the opportunities presented by the African Continental Free Trade Area. By bridging the gap between academic learning and practical industry requirements, we're creating a pipeline of skilled professionals ready to contribute to Africa's economic integration.

Key Objectives

  • Student Empowerment: Equip students with practical knowledge and skills in key economic sectors
  • Industry Linkages: Create direct connections between educational institutions and industry partners
  • Economic Awareness: Foster understanding of AfCFTA opportunities and benefits
  • Career Development: Provide mentorship and career guidance for students

Implementation Strategy

Our implementation began with comprehensive research into the skills gap between academic curricula and industry requirements. We identified key sectors where AfCFTA presents significant opportunities and developed targeted training modules.

The program involves partnerships with educational institutions across Ghana, starting with pilot programs in selected universities and expanding to include second-cycle institutions. Each partnership is customized to address the specific needs and opportunities of the institution and its students.

Strategic Partnerships

We have established collaborations with key stakeholders including:

  • National AfCFTA Coordination Office: Policy guidance and strategic direction
  • Ghana Education Service: Educational framework integration
  • Ghana Export Promotion Authority: Market access and export opportunities
  • National Youth Authority: Youth engagement and mobilization
  • United Nations Youth Association-Ghana: International partnerships and exposure

Impact and Results

Since launch, the initiative has reached over 5,000 students across multiple educational institutions. Participants have reported increased awareness of AfCFTA opportunities and enhanced practical skills in their chosen fields.

The program has successfully facilitated 150+ industry placements and created numerous entrepreneurship opportunities for participating students.

Future Roadmap

Looking ahead, we're expanding the program to include virtual learning platforms and international exchange opportunities. We're also developing specialized tracks for high-demand sectors and establishing an alumni network to ensure continuous engagement and support.

Project Impact Statistics

  • Students Reached: 5,000+
  • Institutions Partnered: 25+
  • Industry Placements: 150+
  • Training Modules: 12
  • Mentorship Sessions: 200+
  • Startups Launched: 30+

CESAP Africa: Cybersecurity Education and Skills Advancement

Launched: June 2021 10 min read

Program Overview

On Friday, 14th June 2024, TECHFARM Hub launched the Cyber Education and Skills Advancement Program (CESAP) Africa at its headquarters in the Eastern Region of Ghana. This landmark event represents a significant step toward advancing cybersecurity education and skills across the African continent, fostering innovation, and building digital resilience.

The program began with an opening prayer led by Alhaji Sulemana, a representative of the Chief Imam, followed by remarks from Dr. Nana Osei Owusu Agyare II, Akwadumhene and Mponuahene, who chaired the event. Dr. Agyare II applauded the initiative and encouraged participants to leverage this platform to enhance their understanding of cybersecurity issues.

CESAP Africa: Vision and Purpose

In a detailed presentation,Mr. Kobina Adomadzi Longdon, Developer and Chief Executive of TECHFARM Hub, outlined the vision behind CESAP Africa. He emphasized the Hubs mission to foster entrepreneurship, innovation, and technological advancement across Africa.

CESAP Africa is designed to train a diverse group of individuals, including:

  • Security personnel (police, fire officers)
  • Journalists
  • Students
  • IT enthusiasts

Target Audience

The program is designed for multiple stakeholder groups:

  • Security Agencies: Law enforcement and national security personnel
  • ICT Professionals: Technology experts seeking cybersecurity specialization
  • Students: The next generation of cybersecurity experts
  • Corporate Sector: Banking, media, and other vulnerable industries

Training Methodology

Our approach combines theoretical knowledge with hands-on practical experience. Participants engage in realistic scenarios and simulations that mirror actual cybersecurity challenges faced by organizations today.

The curriculum is continuously updated to reflect emerging threats and technological advancements, ensuring participants receive the most current and relevant training available.

Certification and Recognition

Participants who successfully complete the program receive internationally recognized certifications that validate their expertise and enhance their professional credentials in the global cybersecurity market.

Impact and Achievements

Since inception, CESAP Africa has trained over 1,000 professionals across various sectors. The program has significantly enhanced Ghana's cybersecurity capabilities and created a growing community of skilled professionals ready to address digital security challenges.

Program Success Metrics

  • Professionals Trained: 1,000+
  • Certifications Issued: 850+
  • Industry Partners: 25+
  • Training Modules: 15
  • Success Rate: 92%
  • Employment Rate: 78%
