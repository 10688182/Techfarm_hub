We're Building The Future of Sustainable Agriculture

TECHFARM Hub is a Pan-African social innovation and ecosystem enabling hub which focuses on entrepreneurship, innovation and business development using disruptive knowledge, tools, skills and resources to upscale activities of entrepreneurs, startups and MSMEs with emphasis on youth, women/girls and Persons With Disabilities.

We stimulate enthusiasm for productive and profitable green, agritech and agribusiness economic opportunities in Ghana and the simulate enthusiasm for productive and profitable, green, agritech, and agribusiness opportunities in Ghana and the diaspora.
